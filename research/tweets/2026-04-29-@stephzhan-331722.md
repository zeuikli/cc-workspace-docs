## 全文

3/ Vibe coding and agentic engineering are not the same thing.

Vibe coding raises the floor.
Agentic engineering raises the ceiling.
One is about access: more people can build.

The other is about excellence: using agents without giving up security, reliability, maintainability, or taste.

The ceiling may be much higher than 10x.

---

## 研究摘要

**TL;DR**：Vibe coding = 存取民主化（底線）；Agentic engineering = 在不犧牲安全/可靠/可維護性的前提下用 agent 追求卓越（天花板），上限可能遠超 10x。

### 核心論點

- 精確區隔：Vibe coding（存取/民主化）vs. Agentic engineering（卓越/工程紀律）
- Agentic engineering 的四個不可妥協項：security、reliability、maintainability、taste
- 天花板預估：遠超 10x（無具體數字，為定性主張）

### 關鍵數據 / 工具

- 四個不可妥協維度：security / reliability / maintainability / taste

### 批判性觀察

- 這個四維框架（security/reliability/maintainability/taste）對 cc-workspace 高度直接可行動：可作為 agent 輸出品質評估的 checklist
- 「The ceiling may be much higher than 10x」是定性主張；cc-workspace 的 deep-review + healthcheck 正是實作 agentic engineering 品質控制的機制
