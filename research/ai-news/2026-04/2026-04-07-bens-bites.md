## [No Claude for Claws](https://www.bensbites.com/p/no-claude-for-claws)
*🍔 Ben's Bites | 2026-04-07*

[Anthropic cut off Claude subscription-funded usage in third-party tools](https://x.com/bcherny/status/2040206440556826908) like OpenClaw. You can still use Claude models through OpenClaw, but it now requires separate pay-as-you-go billing or your own API key — your Claude Code subscription no longer works. Anthropic offered a one-time credit equal to one month’s subscription to soften the blow. The move comes as agentic usage through third-party harnesses was eating massive compute, and Anthropic is clearly steering users toward its own tools: Dispatch, scheduled tasks, projects, and computer use, which directly overlap with what OpenClaw offers. 

But OpenClaw founder, Peter, is a (good-kind of) maniac who’s trying to get gpt5.4 working like opus in the tool (since OpenAI acquired him). 

Unfortunately it’s just left the whole community confused as to where and when they can use their Claude Code subscriptions outside of the cc harness itself. Myself included.

There’s [a new take on knowledge bases](https://x.com/karpathy/status/2039805659525644595) that I think is very interesting by Andrej Karpathy. Memory and file organisation for retrieval got a big focus with the OpenClaw hype but this approach groups things topically and then enhances with summaries, backlinks and wikis. 

Related, Farza built [Farzapedia](https://x.com/FarzaTV/status/2040563939797504467), a personal Wikipedia generated from 2,500 diary entries, notes and messages. Built for his agents to crawl whenever needed. [Karpathy’s take on Farzapedia](https://x.com/karpathy/status/2040572272944324650)

[OpenAI acquired TBPN](https://x.com/johncoogan/status/2039756493621542915) -- good pod to get a peek into the heads of [the hosts](https://x.com/DialecticPod/status/1990452333625446822). A lot of people on X are trying to sound smart with ‘this is why they acquired them’ but I’m more in the Ben Thompson camp… I don’t really know why either party needs each other here? TBPN is loved, growing, and making a small fortune with their (also loved!) ad business. Why does OpenAI need them? Other than the fact that they don’t like traditional media and could use TBPN as their main channel to people that matter.
