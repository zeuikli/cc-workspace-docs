## 全文

5/ AI generated code often works.

That does not mean it is good.

It can be bloated, copy-paste heavy, brittle, and awkwardly abstracted.

The scarce skill is still judgment: knowing when to simplify, when to delete, when the abstraction is wrong, and when "working" is not enough.

---

## 研究摘要

**TL;DR**：AI 生成的程式碼「可以跑」不等於「好的」；稀缺的判斷力是知道何時簡化、何時刪除、何時抽象是錯的。

### 核心論點

- AI 產生程式碼的四個常見缺陷：bloated / copy-paste heavy / brittle / awkwardly abstracted
- 稀缺技能：判斷力 — 「何時簡化」「何時刪除」「何時抽象是錯的」「何時 working 不夠」
- Working ≠ Good：這是 agentic engineering 與 vibe coding 的本質區別之一
